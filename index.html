    BRANDEN CHI

    Art Director
    he/him

    I'm a Korean American Art Director from Delaware, now based in New York City. I'm drawn to work that makes people stop and think, "I wish I thought of that." I care about culture, from cars with family history to comfort food that evolves with us. Those signals shape how I build concepts that feel alive in the real world, not just in a deck. Outside of work, I'm inspired by plants, photography, cars, and learning random skills. I cook often, bake a dangerous chocolate cake, and like making things with my hands. It keeps me curious and keeps my ideas human. I'm opinionated, collaborative, and eager to build courageous work that puts people at the center.
